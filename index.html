Web Technology Tutorial for UCL

Using *SIMPLIFIED* steps we will try to teach you everything a junior web developer should know. After this tutorial and HTML/CSS lessons, you should be able to get a junior front-end developer job! The course is supported by Mozilla & Microsoft.

Previous & future talks

After the previous lectures by Alex Lakatos, Jean-Yves Perrier and of course Dr. Karen Stepanyan you should be confident with:

  • Fundamentals of HTML/CSS;

The main objective of today's talk is to quickly display the process of launching a simple website individually and comparing this to the processes a large organisation would undertake to launch a system. Most of the steps that we go over today will be taught (or were already taught) in more detail later in the course.
